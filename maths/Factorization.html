What is Factorization?

Writing an expression as a product of factors using common factors or formulas.

Key: Find what's common in all terms!

Common Factor Examples

  • 3x + 6 = 3(x + 2)
  • 4a + 8 = 4(a + 2)
  • 2x + 4y = 2(x + 2y)
  • 5ab + 10b = 5b(a + 2)
