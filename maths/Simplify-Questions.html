BODMAS Rule

Brackets → Order → Division → Multiplication → Addition → Subtraction

Always solve in this order!

Examples with Steps

  • 5 + 3 × 2 = 5 + 6 = 11 (× first)
  • (10 + 5) ÷ 3 = 15 ÷ 3 = 5 (brackets first)
  • 2³ + 4 = 8 + 4 = 12 (order/power first)
  • 20 - 5 + 3 = 18 (left to right)

Quick Review

  • Brackets (): Solve first
  • Order ²: Square/square root
  • Divide ÷ and Multiply ×: Left to right
  • Add + and Subtract -: Left to right
