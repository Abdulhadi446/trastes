5. -(x + y) =

Change signs when - is outside

6. -2(x - 2) =

-2×x + (-2)×(-2)

Rules Summary

  • (+) → Remove without changing sign
  • (-) → Change sign of each term
  • n(x + y) → n×x + n×y
  • -n(x - y) → -n×x + n×y

More Examples

  • 5(a + b) = 5a + 5b
  • 3(x - 2) = 3x - 6
  • -(a - b) = -a + b
