PHOTOSYNTHESIS & CHEMICAL REACTIONS

Photosynthesis

Definition: Process by which green plants make food.

Equation: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + Light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

  1. Occurs in chlorophyll
  2. Needs sunlight, water, CO₂
  3. Produces glucose and oxygen

Neutralization

Acid + Base → Salt + Water

Example: HCl + NaOH → NaCl + H₂O

Types of Reactions

  • Combination: A + B → AB
  • Decomposition: AB → A + B
  • Displacement: A + BC → AC + B
  • Double Displacement: AB + CD → AD + CB
