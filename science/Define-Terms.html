DEFINE THE TERMS

Biology

Cell: The smallest unit of life.
Photosynthesis: Process by which plants make food using sunlight.
Respiration: Process of releasing energy from food.
Osmosis: Movement of water through a membrane.
Chromosome: Structure that carries genes.

Chemistry

Atom: Smallest unit of matter.
Molecule: Two or more atoms joined together.
Element: Pure substance made of one type of atom.
Compound: Two or more elements chemically combined.
Oxidation: Reaction with oxygen.

Physics

Force: A push or pull.
Energy: Ability to do work.
Velocity: Speed in a given direction.
Pressure: Force per unit area.
Wave: Transfer of energy through vibration.
