PROPERTIES OF SUBSTANCES

Physical Properties

  • Color: Visual appearance
  • Odour: Smell
  • State: Solid, liquid, or gas
  • Melting Point: Temp. at which solid becomes liquid
  • Boiling Point: Temp. at which liquid becomes gas
  • Solubility: Ability to dissolve in water
  • Density: Mass per unit volume

Chemical Properties

  • Reactivity: How easily it reacts
  • Acidity/Basicity: pH value
  • Flammability: Ability to burn
  • Oxidation: Reaction with oxygen

Examples

  • Water: Colorless, odorless, liquid, soluble
  • Iron: Grey, solid, hard, good conductor
  • Gold: Yellow, shiny, non-reactive
