LETTER / APPLICATION WRITING

Difference Between Letter and Application

  • Letter: Written to friends, family, or relatives. More personal and friendly.
  • Application: Written to principal, headmaster, or authority. Formal and official.

Format for Formal Application

To,
The Headmaster,
[School Name]
[City]

Date: __________

Subject: __________

Respected Sir,

[Your matter/request here]

Thanking you,

Yours faithfully,
[Your Name]
[Class]

Format for Informal Letter

[Your Address]

[Date]

Dear [Name],

[Your message]

Yours lovingly/sincerely,
[Your Name]

Important Phrases

  • I am writing to inform you that...
  • I would like to bring to your kind notice...
  • I request you to grant me leave for...
  • Thanking you in anticipation.
  • I shall be grateful for your kind consideration.

Sample Application: Leave Request

To,
The Headmaster,
St. George's School
City

Date: 15/04/2026

Subject: Leave Application for 3 Days

Respected Sir,

I am a student of Class IX-A. My grandmother is seriously ill and I need to go to Karachi for three days. I request you to grant me leave from 16th to 18th April 2026.

Thanking you,

Yours faithfully,
Ahmed Khan
Class IX-A

Common Application Topics

  • Leave Application (sick leave, urgent work)
  • Fee Concession
  • Issue of Book/Library Card
  • School Transport Facility
  • Change of Section/Class

5 Tips for Good Marks

  1. Write in correct format
  2. Use proper salutations (Respected Sir/Madam)
  3. Keep it short and to the point
  4. Use formal language
  5. Proofread before submitting
