The Generous Farmer

Once in a small village, there lived an elderly farmer named Ali. He was known for his kindness and hard work. Every morning, he would wake up early and work in his fields. He had a beautiful garden full of vegetables and fruits.

One day, a poor traveler came to the village. He had no food and was very hungry. Ali saw him and invited him to his home. He gave the traveler food and a place to stay. The traveler thanked Ali and blessed him with good health.

From that day, Ali's farm became more productive, and he lived happily ever after. The village people learned that kindness brings its own reward.

Questions:

1. Where did the farmer live?

Answer: In a small village

2. What was the farmer known for?

Answer: Kindness and hard work

3. What did Ali give to the traveler?

Answer: Food and a place to stay

4. What lesson did the village learn?

Answer: Kindness brings its own reward
